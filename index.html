    What is Climate Action?



    It refers to efforts and initiatives aimed at reducing greenhouse gas emissions,
    adapting to the impacts of climate change,
    and promoting sustainable practices to protect the environment
    and ensure a livable planet for future generations.

    We should spread awareness about the condition of our climate
    because we definitely feel what climate change
    does to us, especially in our country, the Philippines.

    Take Action

    Take Action!

    Spread Awareness

    Raising awareness about climate action encourages sustainable
    practices and collective efforts to combat climate change for a greener future.

    Plant Trees

    Planting trees is crucial for climate action, as they absorb carbon dioxide,
    improve air quality, restore natural habitats, and promote biodiversity, thereby mitigating climate change effects.

    Apply R-R-R

    By reducing consumption, reusing items, and recycling materials,
    we can create a more sustainable environment and combat climate change effectively.

    Avoid Harmful Substances

    Avoiding harmful substances like plastics, toxic chemicals,
    and fossil fuels helps reduce pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. Choosing sustainable alternatives supports a healthier planet.
